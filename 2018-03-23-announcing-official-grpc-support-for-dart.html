--- title: 'Announcing official gRPC support for Dart' layout: post published: '2018-03-23T04:49:00-07:00' feed: 'Dart News & Updates' link: 'https://news.dartlang.org/2018/03/announcing-official-grpc-support-for.html' author: name: 'Michael Thomsen' email: noreply@blogger.com url: 'https://plus.google.com/112973157970974566545' --- gRPC is a high performance, open source RPC framework. The gRPC framework supports a wide range of languages, and we are happy to announce that support for the Dart language is now available in beta!

Dart gRPC support works with the Dart SDK, version 1.24.3 or higher, and currently supports the Flutter and VM/Server platforms.

See our Medium post for full details.
